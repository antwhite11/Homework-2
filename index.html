About Me

My name is Anthony White. I am a Music Producer from Columbus, Ohio, currently living in Atlanta, GA. I started producing music at the age of 15 and as a result have been to build a successful career. I produce all genres but mainly focus on Hip-Hop, Pop, and R&B. My goal is to make music that inspires the masses.

I decided to dive head first into coding because I felt it was time to learn a new craft. Instead of teaching myself, I enrolled into the Georgia Tech Coding Boot Camp.
